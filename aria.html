ARIA

Aria, the Last Ember — A fierce and battle hardened Reaper, Aria fought in the first war against the gods, standing beside her sisters until she was the only one left. Marked by loss but fueled by vengeance, she commands the mystical power of blue flames — an ethereal fire that burns both flesh and soul. In battle, her flames shape into a spectral bow and blazing arrows, each shot a searing promise to those who wronged her

FLAMESHOT

Deal 30 damage to the enemy hero and apply 1 stack of burn

TRISHOT

Aria fires 3 shots at the enemy hero, dealing 20 damage each

SCORCHED EARTH

Aria releases a barrage of arrows, dealing 90 damage and explodes and stacks of burn.
