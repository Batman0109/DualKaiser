VIKTOR

Viktor, the Crimson Aegis — A royal prince and a formidable Reaper, Viktor stands as the city's strongest line of defense against the gods’ wrath. Bound by duty and honor, he wields the power of crimson energy — summoning red barriers to shield his allies and hurling charged energy blasts to obliterate his foes. His crimson energy blasts pulse with raw power, each strike a testament to his unyielding resolve. Stoic yet fierce, Viktor’s very presence is a warning — the city's defenses are as unbreakable as the prince who guards them
